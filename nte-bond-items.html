Bond Items

Neverness to Everness — every gift, its Affinity value, and its price

Two tiers of gifts exist: universal items every character accepts at max value, and each character's own favorite item.

Full multi-tier gift catalogs (each character actually has 5–10 acceptable gifts at varying Affinity levels) are only published as screenshots I can't reliably read, not text. What's below is the single best confirmed favorite per character — the one guides consistently agree gives the most Affinity for the price. If you want a specific character's full gift list transcribed, send me a screenshot of their in-game Gifts tab and I'll add it here exactly.
Universal Gifts (Accepted by Everyone)
A Handwritten Letter
+2,000 AP
The single best gift in the game — every character treats it as a top favorite. Obtained from the Warp Exchange or Circle Bounty (Battle Pass). Scarce, so save it for characters without an affordable favorite, or for the expensive Level 5–6 thresholds.
Fluffy Cotton
+400 AP
Free and universally accepted. Comes from interacting with the Compressed Boom Boom Cloud anomaly inside your apartment (Wiener Apartments, Eden Apartments, Fenglin Villa). Limited supply — realistically ~20–25 per month.
Ebisu Royal Tower
+100 AP
Free, low value, but not everyone accepts it. Obtained from a story scene at the Ebisu Auction (~40 given). Fine for burning off extras once your priority characters are handled.
