Bond Calculator

Neverness to Everness — how many points, and how long, to reach a target Bond Level

Enter your character's current Bond Level and current Affinity progress within that level, pick a target level, and this works out exactly how much Affinity you still need — plus an estimated timeline based on your daily farming rate.

Per-level Affinity thresholds aren't officially published by the developer — the numbers used here come from a community datamine, and they sum to ~56,500 total for Level 1→10, which lines up closely with the widely-confirmed "~56,000 total Affinity to max a character" figure repeated across most guides. Treat exact per-level numbers as a well-corroborated estimate, not a guaranteed-official source.
