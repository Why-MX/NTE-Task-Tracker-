Fastest Path to Max Bond
1
Max Esper Zero's Five-Star Paragon life skill first — a permanent +5% Affinity bonus on every future gift, to any character.
2
Give favorite gifts daily — 3 to each of your priority characters, using the full 10-gift account-wide cap. Favorite gifts give 100 Affinity vs. only 50 for non-favorites, so never spend a slot on a non-favorite.
3
Focus 3 characters at a time, not your whole roster. With only 10 gifts/day shared across everyone, spreading gifts thin across 10+ characters means nobody reaches max for months. 3 characters × 3 gifts = 9 of your 10 daily gifts, maxing them far faster in sequence.
4
Collect and spend Fluffy Clouds weekly from the Compressed Boom Boom Cloud — don't let them cap out unclaimed.
5
Reply to every heart-icon text message on your in-game phone. These trigger small bond scenes and are free Affinity you'd otherwise miss just by not checking messages.
6
Complete Bond Quests as they unlock in the Bond section — talk to the character while they're present in the city to start them.
7
Pray at the Fortune Shades Prayer Tree daily (Southeast New Homeland District) — costs a Prayer Plaque, grants Affinity to a random character. Low value per use, but free steady progress once per day.
8
Use Home Invitation interactions once unlocked at Bond Level 4 — hugs, photos, rock-paper-scissors, and other My Room activities. Best used to close a small gap right before a level threshold rather than as a primary grind method.
9
Take Swift Travel (taxi) rides — there's a chance an owned character rides along as a passenger, rewarding a small Affinity bonus on a successful trip. Free passive progress, not worth detouring for.
All Ways to Gain Bond Affinity
Gifting
BEST
100 Affinity per favorite gift, 50 for non-favorites. Capped at 3 gifts per character, 10 total per day account-wide.
Heart-Icon Text Messages
GOOD
Characters occasionally text you. Only messages with a heart icon grant Affinity — reply to trigger a short scene or mini-event.
Bond Quests
GOOD
Character-specific story quests found in the Bond section. Unlock as you level, and start by talking to the character in the city. Worth doing for lore too.
Home Invitation / My Room
SITUATIONAL
Unlocks at Bond Level 4. Place a character in My Room for interactions — hugs, photos, rock-paper-scissors. Good for topping off right before a level threshold.
Fluffy Clouds
GOOD
Weekly burst source from the Compressed Boom Boom Cloud. Generated passively based on Apartment Comfort Level — build up your apartment to increase the supply.
Fortune Shades Prayer Tree
SITUATIONAL
Southeast New Homeland District. Buy a Prayer Plaque, pray once per day (night only) for random Affinity to a random character. Low value, but free daily progress.
Swift Travel (Taxi) Rides
SITUATIONAL
A chance an owned character joins as a passenger during a taxi ride, rewarding a small Affinity bonus for that character on completion.
Memory Theater Encounters
PASSIVE
Clearing conversation Encounters unlocks Memory Theater entries and grants Affinity. Some need Main Story, Bond Quests, or Spinoff Quests completed first.
City Hangouts (Dates)
PASSIVE
Unlocked at high Bond thresholds for select characters only — not every character has a unique date encounter yet.
